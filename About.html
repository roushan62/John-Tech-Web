Our Story

Founded in 1998, Industrial Group began as a small scaffolding provider serving local construction projects. Through dedication to quality, safety, and customer service, we've grown into a comprehensive industrial services company with operations across the region.

Today, we employ over 500 skilled professionals and maintain an extensive inventory of equipment to serve the construction, oil & gas, manufacturing, and energy sectors. Our commitment to excellence has made us the preferred partner for many Fortune 500 companies.

Despite our growth, we've maintained our family-owned values and commitment to treating every client project with the utmost importance, whether it's a small facility maintenance contract or a multi-year industrial construction project.
